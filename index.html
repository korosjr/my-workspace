Hi, I’m Abel

Hi, I'm Abel — a software engineering student who treats clean code like poetry and debugging like detective work (minus the trench coat… for now). I’m passionate about building tech that’s not just functional, but feels good — blending logic with a little art, a little heart, and the occasional existential crisis over font choices. Whether it's designing sleek user interfaces or whipping up backend magic, I believe tech should tell a story — preferably one with fewer semicolon errors. Off the screen? I’m either obsessing over FPL, rooting for Lewis Hamilton’s next comeback arc, or reimagining my closet to match my dreamy pilot outfit. This is my digital playground. Pull up a chair, poke around, and if you like what you see — let’s connect.

Hobbies

Art

To me, art isn’t just about brushes and colors — it’s how I translate chaos into calm. Sometimes it’s a sketch on the back of a receipt, other times it’s layers of digital noise turning into a masterpiece at 2 a.m.

Aviation

I don’t just love planes — I live for the roar of the jet engines. I can name aircraft types like people name their exes — with precision, nostalgia, and a hint of drama. If it flies, I’m fascinated. If it crashes… I’ll probably read a 12-page report on it for fun...

Formula 1

It's not just racing — it's Sunday therapy at 300 km/h. I watch for the drama, stay for the strategy, and secretly believe I could’ve been a team principal if life didn’t throw me a degree instead. I can’t afford a Ferrari, but yelling “Forza!” at the screen feels close enough. And yes — I fully believe DRS should be available in traffic.

Coding

Coding is my love language — mostly unreadable, occasionally poetic, and guaranteed to crash at 2 a.m. It’s where I turn caffeine into logic and bugs into existential crises. One minute I'm building the future, the next I'm Googling “why won't my div center.
